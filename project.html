Project Overview

Vendorlution is an e-commerce marketplace designed specifically for students, small businesses, and local vendors. The platform addresses a growing problem faced by students — being scammed on social media marketplaces such as Facebook Marketplace. These scams include sellers disappearing after payment, buyers sending fake proof of payment, and listings that misrepresent the actual product.

Vendorlution introduces a safer transaction model by holding the buyer’s payment in the Vendorlution account until the buyer confirms they have received the item as described.

While secure transactions are a core feature, Vendorlution also addresses broader student needs. It enables students to sell goods and services, generate income, and connect with local buyers and sellers in a trusted environment. This combination of safety and opportunity positions Vendorlution firmly in the domain of Peer-to-Peer (P2P) e-commerce platforms with a student-focused design philosophy.

Project Objectives

  • Provide a secure transaction system to protect students from scams
  • Enable students and small vendors to sell products and services
  • Create a trusted peer-to-peer marketplace tailored for student needs
  • Support local economic activity by connecting students and communities
  • Offer mobile-first responsive design for easy access on all devices

Key Features

  1. Escrow Payments: Buyer’s money is held safely until order is confirmed
  2. Verification System: Reduce fake listings and fraud
  3. Vendor & Student Profiles: Build trust with transparent ratings
  4. Notifications: In-app and email alerts for orders & updates
  5. Mobile-Responsive: Optimized for smartphones, tablets, and PCs
